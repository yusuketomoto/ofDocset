173 standard Kalman filter (in G. Welch' and G. Bishop's notation):
174 
175  x(k)=A*x(k-1)+B*u(k)+w(k) p(w)~N(0,Q)
176  z(k)=H*x(k)+v(k), p(v)~N(0,R)
177 */
